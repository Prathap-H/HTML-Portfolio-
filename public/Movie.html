The Best Movies according to Prathap

My Top 4 Movies of all-time

Anbe Shivam

Anbe Sivam proposes that man can make morally superior choices when he comes face to face with death.

Interstellar

Set in a dystopian future where Earth is suffering from catastrophic blight and famine, the film follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new home for mankind.

Madras

A wall creates discord between two factions of a political party.

Kaali and his friend Anbu, who is a political aspirant, get embroiled in the brutal political rivalry.

Visaranai

Visaranai is a 2015 Tamil movie about four Tamil migrant workers who are tortured and framed for a crime they didn't commit.

The movie is based on the novel Lock Up by M. Chandrakumar.
